📊 QuickBooks Tools to Answer Key Business Questions

Each tool links to a relevant search on Medium for tutorials and deep dives.

Business Question QuickBooks Tool/Report (linked) Insight You Gain
What is impacting profitability? Profit & Loss Report See if income covers expenses and where profit is shrinking.
Profit & Loss by Customer/Job Identify which customers or jobs are profitable vs. unprofitable.
Budget vs. Actual Spot areas where spending exceeded planned budget.
Where is money being spent, and where can savings be found? Expense by Vendor Summary See top vendors where most money goes; negotiate better terms.
Transaction Detail by Account Understand categories (Fuel, Supplies, etc.) driving costs.
Accounts Payable Aging Track unpaid bills to avoid late fees and cash flow strain.
What areas are driving success, and what challenges need attention? Sales by Customer Detail Find top customers driving revenue.
Sales by Product/Service Summary Learn which products/services generate the most income.
Accounts Receivable Aging Spot overdue invoices creating cash flow challenges.
Statement of Cash Flows Check if operations generate enough cash to cover expenses.
How can financial processes become more efficient? Bank Reconciliation Tool Match bank transactions automatically, reduce errors.
Bank Feed Rules Auto-categorize recurring expenses to save time.
Recurring Transactions Automate invoices, bills, and journal entries.
Dashboard (Overview Tab) Get quick snapshots of sales, expenses, and profitability.
How much do you currently owe in taxes, and is your payments up to date? QuickBooks Tools to Analyze Taxes QuickBooks tax tools help track what you owe, confirm what you’ve paid, and prepare accurate records for filing.
What is driving payroll costs, and how can they be managed more efficiently? QuickBooks Tools to Analyze Payroll QuickBooks payroll tools help track wages and taxes, confirm payments, and prepare accurate reports for compliance and decision-making.
